who are youngsters

In the world of cricket, the term "youngsters" often refers to emerging talents who are making a mark in the sport at an early age,
and are seen as potential future stars. These players usually come from various countries and showcase extraordinary skills in
domestic, T20 leagues, or international cricket. Some of them are already starting to make an impact in major international
tournaments.

SHUBMAN GILL

  • International Debut: 2019 (against New Zealand in ODIs)
  • Test Debut: 2020 (against Australia in the Border-Gavaskar Trophy)
  • Gill is known for his elegant stroke play, particularly off the front foot. He plays with soft hands, which allows him to time the ball well.

ARSHDEEP SHING

Arshdeep Singh is an Indian cricketer, known for his left-arm fast-medium bowling. He was born on February 5, 1999, in Amritsar, Punjab, India.
Arshdeep rose to prominence in Indian cricket through his impressive performances in domestic cricket and the Indian Premier League (IPL).
  • Bowling Style: Left-arm fast-medium
  • IPL Team: Punjab Kings (formerly Kings XI Punjab), where he gained recognition for his consistent bowling in the death overs.
  • International Debut: He made his debut for the Indian national team in 2022, showcasing his skill and composure under pressure.
  • Playing Style: Arshdeep is known for his ability to bowl yorkers effectively, especially in the final overs of a T20 match. His accuracy and temperament
    make him a reliable option in limited-overs cricket.
